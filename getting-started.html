So, now that you are motivated to get dirt under your fingernails, cook with your very own fresh herbs, and explore all the joys of urban gardening, how and where do you begin?


The bible of community gardening in NYC is Green Thumb. Operation Green Thumb was initiated in 1979 in response to the city's economic crisis. Today, Green Thumb issues leases to over 550 community gardens in NYC. Green Thumb also provides supplies, resources, training, and workshops on everything relating to gardening and garden organization. Contact Green Thumb to join a garden in your neighborhood.
