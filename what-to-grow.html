What Should I Grow?

Divine

You joined a garden. You have a plot. Your head is filled with images of gorgeous flowers, blooms, annuals, and perennials. You can just taste those mouth-watering fruits, berries, greens, and vegetables. But you are stopped in your tracks. How do you decide what to plant?


First, you need good quality soil. At the start of the garden season, it's a good idea to strip off the top couple of inches of exposed soil, and replace it with fresh soil. Determine how many hours of direct sunlight your plot will receive daily. Most vegetable plants need 6-8 hours, but some shade-loving plants require only 2 hours. Plant accordingly. Don't be too ambitious. Start small and enjoy the process. Check out these resources for great guidance, suggestions, and local workshops. Happy Gardening!
