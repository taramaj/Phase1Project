Resources

Go Garden!

Everything You Need To Get Started!



The bible of community gardens in NYC is Green Thumb.

GrowNYC works to improve quality of life through environmental programs and works closely with Green Thumb.

The American Community Gardening Association supports community gardening across the United States and Canada and has a wealth of information.

The Brooklyn Botanic Garden is a visual inspiration to all gardeners as well as a valuable resource and supporter of community gardening.

Cornell University's Garden Based Learning is an extensive research-based resource. They also provide a curiculum of activities and lessons designed for children and youth.

Kids Gardening is aimed at educating children through school programs and grants, but their website is wonderful and easy to navigate for adult newbies as well!
